Sending a Mail Campaign

Quick Steps Guideline
Messages > Campaigns > Create New Campaign > Email
On the Step 1: Setup page, enter your Campaign Name and select Notification Type as E-Mail.
On the Step 2: What page, choose your email Template and provide details such as Mail Address, Title, and other necessary information.
On the Step 3: Who page, select your target audience.
On the Step 4: When page, set your notification sending time and delivery speed, then click SEND to publish your notification.
